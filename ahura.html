  • Ahura! Project


    Ahura is, to be honest, a warm-up experience for me in smart phone app development. It started around June 2020 when I felt the most angry with myself for the passiveness I had taken on for the past two years. I wanted to do something new, and mobile application was something I had never tried!

    Ahura is a stock management application. It helps you to keep track of your stocks and make faster and easier decision for stock exchange. Ahura works as an assistant who keeps the information you need of the stocks you own and the stocks you're looking to buy in tables and figure.

    I wish to release Ahura open-source. I hope I can create a friendly network of stock traders to socialize and share and benefit from each other's experiences.

    It is written is Javascript(!), with React-Native. I'm expecting to start the alpha test in the first week of September 2020. Fingers crossed, the Beta test will start in October 2020. I'm planning on immediately releasing the source code and look for assistance from coding enthusiasts. Simultaneously, I will work on social networking features and get a server running.
